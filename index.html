Our Mission & Leadership

Vision & Founding Story

Searcha was founded after seeing firsthand how great businesses struggle when they’re invisible.

Garlen Woest, one of our co-founders, worked with his father who owned a sushi restaurant and constantly faced the same challenge of finding reliable, high-quality chefs who would stay. The issue wasn’t the job. It was visibility. The right talent simply wasn’t discovering the opportunity.

At the same time, Luka Pranjeta, a computer science and AI specialist, was studying how modern search was changing how people now find businesses, jobs, and services through AI, maps, and digital platforms, not just Google.

Together, they built a visibility-first system designed to make brands impossible to miss.

Today, Searcha helps dental practices grow by increasing brand visibility to attract better patients and a stronger talent pool at the same time. When your practice is visible everywhere people search, growth follows.
